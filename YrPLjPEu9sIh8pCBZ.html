AI in Fraud Detection: MENA Perspectives

Date & Time

June 15, 2023 | 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM GST

Duration

90 minutes

Language

About the Webinar

Join us for an insightful exploration of how artificial intelligence is revolutionizing fraud detection in the Middle East and North Africa (MENA) region. This webinar will delve into the latest AI technologies and their applications in combating financial crime, with a specific focus on the unique challenges and opportunities within the MENA context.

Our panel of experts will discuss:

  • Current trends in AI-powered fraud detection
  • Case studies from leading financial institutions in the MENA region
  • Regulatory considerations and compliance challenges
  • Future outlook and emerging technologies
