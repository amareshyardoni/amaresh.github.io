About MENA Fraud

Our Mission

At MENA Fraud, we are dedicated to safeguarding businesses and individuals across the Middle East and North Africa region from the ever-evolving threat of fraud. Our mission is to provide cutting-edge fraud prevention solutions, expert consultancy, and comprehensive education to empower organizations in their fight against financial crime.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a team of cybersecurity experts and former law enforcement professionals, MENA Fraud has quickly become the leading authority on fraud prevention in the MENA region. We recognized the unique challenges faced by businesses operating in this diverse and rapidly growing market and set out to create tailored solutions that address these specific needs.

Over the years, we have expanded our services to include online webinars, job placement for fraud prevention professionals, and the development of state-of-the-art fraud prevention tools. Our commitment to innovation and excellence has earned us the trust of major financial institutions, e-commerce platforms, and government agencies across the region.

Our Approach

We believe that effective fraud prevention requires a multi-faceted approach. That's why we offer:

  • Cutting-edge technology solutions
  • Comprehensive training and education programs
  • Expert consultancy services
  • Networking opportunities for fraud prevention professionals
  • Up-to-date information on the latest fraud trends and prevention techniques
