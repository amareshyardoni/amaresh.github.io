AI in Fraud Detection: Revolutionizing Financial Security in MENA

Webinar Details

  • Date: June 15, 2023
  • Time: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM (Gulf Standard Time)
  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Platform: Zoom (link will be sent upon registration)
  • Language: English (Arabic subtitles available)
  • Cost: Free

About the Webinar

Join us for an insightful webinar exploring the transformative power of Artificial Intelligence in fraud detection across the Middle East and North Africa region. As financial institutions and businesses face increasingly sophisticated fraud attempts, AI-powered solutions are becoming indispensable in the fight against financial crime.

What You'll Learn

  • The current landscape of fraud in the MENA region
  • How AI and machine learning are revolutionizing fraud detection
  • Real-world case studies of AI implementation in MENA financial institutions
  • Challenges and considerations in adopting AI for fraud detection
  • Future trends and predictions for AI in financial security
  • Best practices for integrating AI into existing fraud prevention strategies

Who Should Attend

  • Fraud prevention professionals
  • Risk management executives
  • Compliance officers
  • IT security specialists
  • Financial technology innovators
  • Banking and finance professionals
  • Regulatory authorities
